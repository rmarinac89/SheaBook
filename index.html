About the book.

When parents divorce, it can feel like your whole world has come crashing down. You might believe you’re the victim and the cause - confused and lost. Overwhelmed beyond belief, where do you go? What can you do?

My Parents are Getting Divorced...Now What? is a guide for you, the youth of families going through divorce. With a focus on maintaining health and happiness, this book helps you establish the foundation for success in your own life during such a traumatizing family situation. When things fall apart, the right tools will help you rebuild... and rise above.

About the Author.

My own experience with the divorce of my parent’s is something I struggled with for years until I began to make drastic changes in my life, changes that allowed me to come to terms with the situation and live a happy and healthy lifestyle.

The opportunity to help others get through this time in their life is something that I do not take for granted. I am confident that I have created a tool that will help guide youth through some of the most important years of their lives, while at the same time overcoming the obstacle that their parent’s divorce can be.
